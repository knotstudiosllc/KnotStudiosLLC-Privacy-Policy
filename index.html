Privacy Policy

Last updated April 22, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

Overview

This application is developed and published by KnotStudios LLC. This policy describes what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used and protected.

This policy applies to all software products, games, and services developed by KnotStudios LLC that are made available through the Meta Horizon Store or any other distribution platform.

Data We Collect

We collect the minimum data required to operate the application. This includes:

  • Your Meta/Oculus user ID and display name, provided by the Meta platform upon login
  • Session tokens used to authenticate your requests to our backend services
  • In-app purchase and transaction records, including SKUs purchased and timestamps
  • In-game inventory and wallet balance associated with your account
  • IP address and user agent at time of login, used for session security
  • Display name changes made within the application

We do not collect your real name, email address, phone number, or any payment card details. Payment processing is handled entirely by Meta.

How We Use Your Data

  • To authenticate your identity via Meta's platform and maintain your session
  • To store and synchronize your in-game progress, inventory, and currency balance
  • To verify in-app purchases server-side and credit your account accordingly
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity
  • To deliver real-time in-game messages and events

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the operation of the application.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial purposes. Data is shared only as follows:

  • Meta Platforms, Inc. — to verify purchases, entitlements, and user identity via Meta's Graph API
  • Supabase, Inc. — our backend database and authentication infrastructure provider, operating under their own privacy and data processing policies

Both third parties are used solely to operate core application functionality and are bound by their respective data processing agreements.

Data Retention

Session tokens expire after 24 hours of inactivity. Expired nonces used for authentication are automatically deleted from our systems.

Account data, including inventory and transaction history, is retained for the lifetime of your account.

Data Deletion

You may request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing privacy@knotstudios.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will process your request within 30 days and confirm once deletion is complete.

Upon a valid deletion request, we will permanently remove your account, session records, inventory, wallet balance, transaction history, and any other personal data associated with your user ID from our systems.

We will never charge a fee for data deletion requests. In some cases, we may be required to retain certain records for legal or fraud prevention purposes — if this applies to your request, we will inform you and explain why.

Security

We implement the following measures to protect your data:

  • All communication between the application and our servers is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Session tokens are 256-bit cryptographically random values
  • Authentication is performed server-side using Meta's official verification APIs
  • Row-level security is enforced on all database tables
  • IP blacklisting is applied to known malicious address ranges at login

Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the application after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
